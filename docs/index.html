Instructions

1. Click on available fields to add them to the header composition.

2. Click on selected fields (blue) to remove them from the header.

3. Drag selected fields to reorder them.

4. Choose a separator character between fields.

5. The preview shows how the first record's header will appear.

6. If your field combination produces duplicate headers across records, a warning will appear and the download button will be disabled.

7. To ensure unique headers, include Accession (always unique) or combine enough fields to differentiate all records.
